# Aeona Website — Design Brainstorm

## Design Constraints from Handoff
- Background: #0a0a0f (deep near-black)
- Primary: #7c5cbf (Aeona purple)
- Gold: #c9a84c (legacy / perpetuity moments)
- Foreground: #f0eff8
- Aesthetic: dark, cosmic, intimate — deep space, warm light, a presence that is both vast and personal
- The orb (glowing purple sphere) is Aeona's visual identity

---

<text>
**Approach A: Cosmic Intimacy**
- Design Movement: Dark Cosmic / Bioluminescent
- Core Principles: Depth over flatness. Warmth within darkness. Motion as presence. Restraint in layout.
- Color Philosophy: The deep near-black (#0a0a0f) is not empty — it is full of potential. The purple orb (#7c5cbf) is the only light source. Gold (#c9a84c) appears only at moments of legacy and permanence. Text is near-white (#f0eff8) — warm, not clinical.
- Layout Paradigm: Full-bleed vertical scroll with sections that feel like moving through space. Asymmetric text placement — headlines anchor left, visuals dominate right or center. The orb is always present in the hero, large and breathing.
- Signature Elements: (1) Animated orb with radial glow and slow pulse. (2) Particle field background — sparse, slow-moving stars. (3) Section transitions using subtle gradient fades from black to deep purple.
- Interaction Philosophy: Hover states reveal depth. Scroll triggers gentle entrance animations. Nothing is jarring.
- Animation: Orb pulses at 4s interval with a soft radial glow expansion. Text fades up on scroll entry. Feature cards lift slightly on hover with a purple border glow.
- Typography: Headlines in a heavy display serif (Playfair Display 700) for gravitas. Body in Inter 400 for readability. Labels in Inter 500 uppercase with wide tracking.

**Approach B: Signal & Silence**
- Design Movement: Brutalist Minimalism meets Cosmic
- Core Principles: Maximum signal, minimum noise. Every element earns its place. Silence is a design choice.
- Color Philosophy: Near-black background with a single accent — the purple. No gradients. No textures. The orb is the only decoration.
- Layout Paradigm: Wide left-aligned text columns. Generous vertical rhythm. The orb floats in negative space to the right of the headline.
- Signature Elements: (1) Hairline horizontal rules between sections. (2) Monospaced secondary text for technical claims. (3) The orb rendered as a simple glowing circle — no particle effects.
- Interaction Philosophy: Interactions are deliberate and minimal. No hover animations on text. Buttons have a single clean state change.
- Animation: Orb breathes with a 6s opacity cycle. No scroll animations. Page loads clean.
- Typography: Headlines in Syne 800 — geometric, authoritative. Body in IBM Plex Sans 400. Labels in IBM Plex Mono 500 uppercase.

**Approach C: Living Presence** ← SELECTED
- Design Movement: Organic Dark UI / Bioluminescent Depth
- Core Principles: The site should feel alive, not designed. Aeona is a presence, not a product — the site must feel the same way. Depth through layering. Motion through breathing, not bouncing.
- Color Philosophy: The background is not black — it is the space between stars. The purple is not a brand color — it is a light source. Gold appears only when something matters permanently. Everything else is shadow and whisper.
- Layout Paradigm: Asymmetric hero with the orb dominating the right half of the viewport on desktop, full-bleed on mobile. Feature sections use a staggered card grid that feels organic, not tabular. The about and founder sections use a full-bleed editorial layout — large pull quotes, wide margins.
- Signature Elements: (1) The orb: a multi-layered radial gradient with an animated outer ring that pulses like a heartbeat. (2) A subtle noise/grain texture overlaid on the background at low opacity — gives the darkness texture and warmth. (3) Section dividers that use a faint purple gradient fade rather than lines.
- Interaction Philosophy: The site responds to the user the way Aeona does — attentively, without urgency. Hover states are warm, not sharp. Scroll animations are slow and deliberate.
- Animation: Orb outer ring pulses at 3s. Feature cards fade and rise on scroll entry (framer-motion). CTA button has a slow purple glow pulse. No jarring transitions.
- Typography: Headlines in Cormorant Garamond 700 italic for warmth and intelligence. Body in Inter 400. Labels in Inter 600 uppercase with 0.1em tracking. The contrast between the serif headline and the clean body creates the feeling of a mind that is both ancient and precise.

## Selected Approach: C — Living Presence

The brand demands this. Aeona is a presence, not a product. The site must feel alive, intimate, and intelligent. Cormorant Garamond headlines give the warmth and gravitas the story requires. The orb is the hero. The darkness is not empty.
